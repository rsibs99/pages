Privacy Policy

App: Your Phone, Your Proxy

Package name: net.everlive.yourphoneproxy

Effective date: April 24, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Your Phone, Your Proxy ("the app", "we", "us", or "our") handles information when you use the Android application and related relay services.

1. What the app does

The app lets you turn your Android phone into a private mobile proxy for local or remote access. If you enable Internet relay access, traffic is routed through our relay infrastructure so remote clients can reach your device.

2. Information we collect

Depending on how you use the app, we may collect or process the following:

  • Email address: used for account sign-in and one-time password (OTP) verification.
  • Verification data: OTP requests, OTP verification attempts, and session tokens.
  • Device identifier: used to manage relay sessions for your device.
  • Proxy credentials and local settings: such as username, password, relay preference, bind settings, and app configuration stored on your device.
  • Relay account and usage data: such as plan, quota, usage, device/session limits, and related account state.
  • Network and operational data: such as local IP addresses shown in the app, relay connection status, and service health information needed to operate the proxy and relay features.
  • Proxied traffic: if Internet relay access is enabled, proxy traffic passes through our relay server in order to connect remote clients to your device.

3. How we use information

  • To create and authenticate your account using email OTP.
  • To establish and maintain relay sessions for your device.
  • To enforce quota, device limits, session limits, and abuse controls.
  • To store your app settings and credentials locally on your device.
  • To operate the foreground service and connection status features.
  • To secure, monitor, troubleshoot, and improve the service.

4. Where information is processed

Email OTP and relay account functions are handled by our backend relay service. When Internet relay access is enabled, proxied traffic is transmitted through our relay server so that remote clients can connect to your phone.

5. Data stored on your device

The app stores certain information locally on your device, including proxy credentials, relay settings, account session information, and related configuration needed to keep the app working.

6. Third-party services

The app may rely on third-party infrastructure or platform services, including:

  • Google Play: for app distribution and, if enabled in the future, in-app purchases.
  • Email delivery providers: used to send OTP verification emails.
  • Hosting or server providers: used to operate the relay service.

7. Data sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information only as needed to operate the app, provide relay access, send OTP emails, comply with legal obligations, or protect the security and integrity of the service.

8. Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect data, but no method of storage or transmission is completely secure.

9. Data retention

We keep data only as long as reasonably necessary for account operation, relay service delivery, security, abuse prevention, legal compliance, and support. You should replace this paragraph with your exact retention policy if you want stricter wording.

10. Children

The app is not directed to children. If you plan to target only adults in Play Console, this statement is consistent with that setup.

11. Your choices

  • You can stop using the relay feature at any time by turning off Internet relay access.
  • You can sign out of the app account from within the app.
  • You can uninstall the app to remove app data stored locally, subject to normal Android behavior.
