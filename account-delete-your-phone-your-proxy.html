Account and Data Deletion

App: Your Phone, Your Proxy

Package name: net.everlive.yourphoneproxy

Developer: RSI Business Solutions

This page explains how users of Your Phone, Your Proxy can delete their app account and associated data.

How to delete your account

  1. Open the Your Phone, Your Proxy app on the Android device where you are signed in.
  2. Go to the Account section.
  3. Tap Delete account and confirm the prompt.

Your account is deleted after you confirm the action in the app.

What will be deleted

  • App account associated with your email address
  • Active app session records
  • Associated relay account records that are no longer required to operate or secure the service

What may be retained temporarily

Certain limited records may be retained for a reasonable period where necessary for security, fraud prevention, abuse prevention, legal compliance, or technical troubleshooting.

If you cannot access the app

If you no longer have access to the signed-in device, contact rsi_bs@yahoo.com from the account email address used in the app and request account deletion assistance.

Other data deletion requests

Separate requests to delete some data without deleting the account are not currently supported.
