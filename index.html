Glucomoni

Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how the Glucomoni Android app, published by RSI Business Solutions, handles information when you use the app.

Effective date: April 21, 2026

Glucomoni is a health-related logging app. It is not a medical device and does not diagnose, treat, cure, or prevent any disease or condition.

1. Summary

Glucomoni is designed to keep your readings on your device by default. The app does not require an account and does not automatically upload your glucose log, notes, tags, or exports to Glucomoni servers.

Some optional features use external services, such as sponsored product content, voice dictation provided by your device or another app, and sharing or emailing that you start yourself.

2. Information Stored on Your Device

Glucomoni may store the following information locally on your device:

  • Blood glucose readings and timestamps
  • Selected ritual or meal context, tags, and notes
  • Language preference and app settings
  • App lock settings
  • Gestational mode preference
  • Backup recovery code used for optional encrypted backups

This information is stored on your device so the app can provide logging, trends, filtering, and export features.

3. Information We Do Not Require

  • No Glucomoni account is required to use the app.
  • We do not ask you to sign in.
  • We do not provide cloud sync in the current version.
  • We do not use in-app analytics or crash reporting tools in the current version.

4. Sponsored Content and Network Requests

Glucomoni may show an optional sponsored product banner. To load that content, the app sends a request to a Glucomoni-controlled sponsored content service.

The request may include:

  • A shared app credential used to authorize the request
  • A two-letter country code derived from your device locale when available
  • Standard network metadata that service providers may receive automatically, such as IP address, request headers, and timestamps

The sponsored content service may use infrastructure and upstream commerce-content providers to return one sponsored product result. If you tap a sponsored link, you leave the app and continue under the third party's terms and privacy practices.

Glucomoni does not use your glucose readings, notes, tags, or exports to personalize sponsored content.

5. Optional Voice Input

Glucomoni offers optional voice dictation for notes. When you start voice input, the app opens the speech recognition flow provided by Android or another app on your device.

Voice audio and transcription may be processed by your device manufacturer, Google, or another speech recognition provider, depending on what is installed on your device. That processing is governed by that provider's privacy practices, not this Privacy Policy.

6. Optional Exports, Sharing, and Email

Glucomoni lets you create PDF or CSV exports and share them using Android's share sheet. These actions only happen when you choose to do them.

  • Export files are generated on your device.
  • If you share an export, the receiving app or service handles that file under its own privacy policy.
  • If you tap the support email action, your email app handles the message and any information you include.

7. Optional Encrypted Backup and Restore

Glucomoni can create an encrypted backup file when you choose to do so. Backup files are encrypted before being written to the location you select.

The app does not automatically upload backups to Glucomoni servers. You are responsible for the storage location you choose and for safeguarding your recovery code.

8. How We Use Information

Glucomoni uses information to:

  • Save and display your log entries on your device
  • Generate trends, summaries, filters, and exports
  • Support optional security features such as app lock and encrypted backup
  • Load optional sponsored content
  • Open optional support and sharing flows that you start

9. Data Sharing

Glucomoni does not sell your health data.

We do not automatically share your glucose readings, notes, tags, or exports with Glucomoni servers. Information may be disclosed to third parties only in the limited situations below:

  • When the app requests optional sponsored content from service providers
  • When you use optional voice dictation provided by another app or platform service
  • When you choose to share an export or send an email
  • When disclosure is required by law, regulation, legal process, or enforceable governmental request

10. Data Retention and Deletion

Information stored by the app remains on your device until you edit it, delete it, uninstall the app, clear app storage, or overwrite it by restoring another backup.

Because Glucomoni does not currently require accounts or cloud storage, there is no Glucomoni-hosted account data for you to delete in the current version.

11. Security

We use reasonable measures intended to protect information handled by the app, including on-device storage, Android platform protections, optional app lock, and encrypted backup functionality. No method of storage, transmission, or device security is perfect, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

12. Children's Privacy

Glucomoni is not designed for children to use on their own. If you believe personal information from a child has been provided through optional support or sharing channels, contact us so we can review the situation.

13. International Use

If you use Glucomoni outside your home country, your information may be processed by third-party services in other countries when you use optional features such as sponsored content, speech recognition, email, or file sharing.

14. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will update the effective date on this page.
